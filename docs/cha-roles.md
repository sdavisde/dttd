# CHA Roles

Each team member on a Tres Dias weekend is assigned a **CHA role** that defines their specific responsibilities for that weekend. A volunteer may have a different role on the Men's weekend versus the Women's weekend, or serve on only one of the two.

Some roles are prefixed with **Head** — these are the leads for their respective teams.

Rollos (prepared talks) are assigned to specific roles and tracked in `weekend_roster.rollo`. See the [Rollos](#rollos) section below.
